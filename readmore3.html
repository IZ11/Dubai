Dubai Desert




General Information


Desert Safari Dubai is an authentic Arabian adventure that lasts for about 4 hours to 6 hours. Overflowing with invigorating fun moments and jaw-dropping thrills, this exciting outing deep into the desert is a genuine must-do during your Dubai holiday. The best thing is that there is a desert safari tour perfect for every ability or preference no matter you’re an early riser or an ardent nyctophile. Just select from our morning, evening or overnight desert safaris. A short drive of just 30 to 40 minutes will retreat you to the otherworldly desert of Dubai; this allows you to admire the stunning contrasts of the city’s urbanscape and supremely isolated sand dunes. At first glance, the desert environs may appear to be arid, barren and uninhabited for your untrained eyes. But that’s not actually the reality as our one-off desert safari unfolds before you its ethereal magnificence and awe-inspiring diversity on a grand scale.
